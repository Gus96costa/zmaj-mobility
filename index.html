The Heritage

Plečnik’s Vision

Since 2007, Ljubljana’s historical center has been a car-free ecological zone of 12 hectares. Zmaj is the technological infrastructure designed to respect Jože Plečnik’s human-scale architecture, providing silent, zero-emission mobility for the soul of the city.
02

The Urban Pulse

Digital Rhythm

A smart, invisible skeleton powers the city. The Zmaj network breathes life into urban commutes. Scan effortlessly, ride silently, and dock seamlessly at illuminated Zmaj Spots spread across town. Digital convenience meets physical freedom.

03

The Green Symbiosis

Social Impact

As the first EU capital to commit to a Zero Waste vision, Ljubljana relies on Zmaj to bridge the gap between urban growth and environmental preservation. By neutralizing carbon footprints and restoring the city’s natural silence, we ensure that the only pulse felt along the Ljubljanica banks is the rhythm of life itself.
